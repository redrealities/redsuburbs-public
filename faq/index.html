Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Were is the map data coming from?
A: Crime data sourced from Queensland Police Service public dataset and population data comes from 2016 Census.

Q: How is Crime Rank calculated?
A: Crime Rank is based on number of crimes and their severity proportionate to the suburb population.

Q: Over which period is the data shown?
A: Crime data is annual QPS 2021 data and population data is from 2016 Census.

Q: I see a mistake, can you fix it?
A: Yes, please report it with "Send Feedback" button or just email to info@redrealities.com!
